# 3D Printing on OpenBSD

**or** how to port python apps to OpenBSD!

---

## Overview of 3D Printing

  - Modeling (or finding) what you are going to print.
  - Exporting to printer friendly formats (stl or gcode).
  - Slicing.
  - Printing.

---
